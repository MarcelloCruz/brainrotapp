# Vision: Dopamine Tax
**Core Philosophy:** Technology should empower human intention, not exploit human instinct. Dopamine Tax is a premium digital wellbeing tool that utilizes behavioral economics (loss aversion) to intercept doomscrolling seamlessly.

## The Experience
* **System-Level Interventions (Full Block):** When a user launches a dedicated short-form app (e.g., TikTok), they are met with a fluid, frosted-glass overlay. They can gracefully exit back to the Home Screen ("Cancel"), or consciously choose to spend a micro-transaction to unlock a timed session.
* **Surgical Interventions (Tab Block):** For mixed-media apps (Instagram, YouTube), the intervention is precise. Tapping the "Reels" or "Shorts" tab triggers an immediate, seamless redirection to the safe Home feed, accompanied by a polite financial prompt. 

## Technical Foundation
* **Framework:** Flutter (Dart), utilizing highly native-feeling components (Cupertino styling for iOS, refined Material 3 for Android).
* **Native Bridging:** Flawless integration with iOS Family Controls (Screen Time API) and Android Accessibility Services.
* **Privacy-First:** User data never leaves the device unless strictly necessary for wallet synchronization. All behavioral tracking is processed locally.
